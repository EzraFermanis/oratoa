Health Services

Kia Ora - Welcome To Ora Toa Health Services

Our Mission is to assist the Porirua and wider communities to be aware of Health issues, by providing information, options and choices which will empower maori and non maori to develop and Maintain a healthy lifestyle Ora Toa is committed to working within the bounds of Tikanga O Toa Rangatira.

Services Provided

Tamariki Ora

Our Registered Nurses and Community Health workers are available to assist and support all.

Whanau Ora

Caring for whanau through the lifespan, from Tamariki to Kaumatua.

Alcohol & Drug Services

We help with problem drug and alcohol addictions. Getting you back on track to live a healthy, happy life.

Asthma Service

GP (Doctor) Clinic
